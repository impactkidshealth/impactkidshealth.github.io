About IMPACT

Building a shared foundation for pediatric cellular therapy discovery

The IMPACT Consortium brings together pediatric transplant and cellular therapy centers to harmonize immune monitoring, microbiome profiling, and clinical outcome data across institutions.

By aligning assays, sample processing, data structures, and analytical pipelines, IMPACT creates multi-center datasets that can reveal how immune recovery shapes complications such as graft-versus-host disease, relapse, infection, and long-term treatment response.

Our mission

IMPACT accelerates discovery in pediatric HCT and CAR-T by transforming center-specific measurements into harmonized, reproducible, multi-omics datasets that can support biomarker discovery, predictive modeling, and future risk-adapted therapeutic strategies.

What makes IMPACT different

A consortium built for harmonized discovery

Shared experimental standards

Participating centers use harmonized standard operating procedures and aligned immune-monitoring workflows to support cross-center comparison.

Matched multi-omics platforms

IMPACT integrates spectral flow cytometry, Olink proteomics, microbiome profiling, and clinical data within a common translational framework.

Pediatric cellular therapy focus

The consortium is designed around pediatric HCT and CAR-T, where immune recovery, toxicity, relapse, and long-term outcomes require coordinated multi-center study.
