IMPACT Consortium

Advancing Precision Immune Monitoring in Pediatric Cellular Therapy

A multi-center partnership integrating immune profiling, microbiome sequencing, and clinical outcomes to improve transplant and cellular therapy outcomes in children.

  • 6 world-leading pediatric centers
  • Multi-omics datasets across HCT, BMT, and CAR-T
  • Harmonized spectral flow, Olink, and microbiome pipelines
  • Standardized protocols for sample processing, storage, and analysis
  • Integrated immune, microbiota, genetic, and clinical outcome data
