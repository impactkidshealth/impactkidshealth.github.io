IMPACT Consortium

Immune & Microbiota Monitoring Partnership for Cellular Therapy

A cross-institution partnership integrating immune profiling, microbiota sequencing, and clinical outcomes to advance precision medicine for children undergoing transplant and cell therapy.

  • 6 world-leading pediatric centers
  • Harmonized spectral flow, Olink, and microbiome pipelines
  • Multi-omics datasets across HCT, BMT, CAR-T
  • Standardized protocols for sample processing, storage, and analysis
  • First consortium to integrate immune + microbiota + genetics + outcomes at scale
