Harmonized immune recovery monitoring after HCT: evidence and practical guidance from the Westhafen Intercontinental Group

Hammoudi T, Nucera S, Troullioud Lucas AG, Ansari M, Balduzzi A, Bertaina A, Buechner J, Corbacioglu S, Dalle JH, Kalwak K, Lee D, Levine JE, Lindemans CA, Locatelli F, Meisel R, Nierkens S, Ottaviano G, Perez-Martinez A, Pichler H, Prockop SE, Pulsipher MA, Talano JM, Tewari S, Schultz KR, Shah NN, Verneris MR, Boelens JJ.

This review proposes evidence-based guidelines for immune reconstitution (IR) monitoring after allogeneic transplant, focusing on key immune milestones such as CD4+ T-cell recovery and harmonized flow cytometry panels. By standardizing IR assessments and identifying modifiable risk factors, multicenter transplant programs can improve patient stratification and clinical outcomes.

Blood Advances (2025) | doi: 10.1182/bloodadvances.2025016915 | PMID: 40902074

Immune Monitoring after Cell Therapy and Hematopoietic Cell Transplantation: Guidelines by the ISCT Stem Cell Engineering Committee

Oppizzi L, Hosszu K, Prockop S, Bidgoli A, Bonfim C, Ciccocioppo R, Ruggeri A, Maiers M, Bertaina A, Boelens JJ.

This review provides practical guidance for building immune monitoring programs in transplant and cellular therapy settings, emphasizing the use of blood-based biomarkers to track immune reconstitution and predict relapse or toxicity. Harmonized approaches may improve early detection and clinical decision-making beyond traditional diagnostics.

Cytotherapy (2025) | doi: 10.1016/j.jcyt.2025.04.069 | PMID: 40493000
